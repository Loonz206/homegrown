Who is this guy?

Ever wake up in the morning to looking forward to building a website that works on phones, tablets and desktops? I do.

Incredibly focused on making iterations and designs for the entire web experince, I use methods that are tried and true to implementing web experinces from the mobile to the desktop. A firm believer in working closely with development and stakeholders the best means to implement concepts is working together on a project as a team.

Following the latest blogs and articles on RWD and Progressive enchancement I am a avid believer that the world we live in is evolving from tradtional buisness models and it is up to us to convey how to approach needs and buisness requirements in the best model possible by making mistakes often and early to replicate the best model going forward. Test early, test often.
